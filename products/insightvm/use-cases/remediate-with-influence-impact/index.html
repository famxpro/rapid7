Break Down Silos Across Teams and Make Shared Progress

InsightVM is not a silver bullet. Instead, it provides the shared view and common language needed to align traditionally siloed teams and drive impact. It also supports a proactive approach to vulnerability management with tracking and metrics that create accountability for remediators, demonstrate impact across teams, and celebrate progress.

Enable better communication with IT and DevOps

Nobody likes wading through spreadsheets and email attachments to figure out what to do. InsightVM lets you know specific actions to take to reduce the most risk, and integrates with ticketing solutions to provide easy remediation instructions to IT and DevOps teams—all while letting your security team track remediation progress in real time.

Track and measure your impact

Track the goals and metrics most relevant and impactful to your team with Goals and SLAs in InsightVM. Then, communicate (and celebrate) those milestones with other technical teams and leadership through customizable Live Dashboards and reporting.

Embed security into other teams' processes

It's much more cost effective to fix security flaws before deployment. InsightVM lets you assess containers to ensure apps are secure before they go into production, and the Rapid7 Insight Agent helps infrastructure teams automatically assess new cloud infrastructure as soon as it goes live.

Accelerate innovation securely

Modern infrastructure like cloud services and containers enables incredible innovation, but causes headaches for security. InsightVM seamlessly integrates with these new types of devices to give you instant insight into the risks they pose, so you don't have to compromise security to move fast.

Tas Giakouminakis

Co-Founder & Chief Technology Officer

Tas leads the development and integration of Rapid7’s award-winning solutions, driving the technical direction to enable customers through quality, simplicity, and innovation. Prior to founding Rapid7, Tas helped form Percussion Software, where he led the development of Percussion's first product. He has also developed software in the security and risk areas for CitiCorp.

Lee Weiner

Chief Product Officer

View Bio

Lee Weiner

Chief Product Officer

Lee Weiner brings a wealth of security and product leadership experience to Rapid7, where he is responsible for leading the directions and delivery of Rapid7's entire product portfolio. Before becoming Chief Product Officer, Lee was VP of Products at LogMeIn, Inc., a provider of cloud-based remote connectivity solutions. He has also held leadership roles at several software security firms, including Netegrity, Inc., IMlogic, Inc., and Symantec Corporation. Lee received a B.A. from the University of Massachusetts.

Bob Rudis

View Bio

Bob Rudis

Chief Data Scientist

Bob Rudis has over 20 years of experience using data to help defend global Fortune 100 companies and is currently [Master] Chief Data Scientist at Rapid7, where he specializes in research on internet-scale exposure. He was formerly a Security Data Scientist & Managing Principal at Verizon, overseeing the team that produces the annual Data Breach Investigations Report. Bob is a serial tweeter (@hrbrmstr), avid blogger (rud.is), R (#rstats) avunculur, author (Data-Driven Security), speaker, and regular contributor to the open source community.

Derek Abdine

Senior Director, R7Labs

View Bio

Derek Abdine

Senior Director, R7Labs

Derek Abdine has been making the internet a safer place for over a decade. Hacker at heart, security researcher by trade, Derek applies his background in packet dissection, plaintext and binary protocols, and dynamic and static code patching to shine light on vulnerabilities; his research has been disclosed both publicly and privately with Fortune 500 companies. Most recently, Derek has been leading the charge against global cyber security threats as Senior Director of Rapid7 Labs, where he and his team collect and analyze over 3.6 billion IP addresses to share insights with the infosec community. He also enjoys hacking games. If you’ve played Grand Theft Auto on multiplayer, you’ve seen his handiwork.

Tod Beardsley

Research Director

Tod Beardsley is the director of research at Rapid7. He has over 20 years of hands-on security experience, stretching from in-band telephony switching to modern IoT implementations. He has held IT Ops and IT Security positions in large organizations such as 3Com, Dell, and Westinghouse, as both an offensive and defensive practitioner. Today, Tod directs the myriad security research programs and initiatives at Rapid7. He can be uniquely identified at https://keybase.io/todb.

Deral Heiland

Research Lead, IoT

View Bio

Deral Heiland

Research Lead, IoT

Deral Heiland, CISSP, has over 20 years of experience in Information Technology, and has held multiple positions including: Senior Network Analyst, Network Administrator, Database Manager, Financial Systems Manager and Senior Information Security Analyst. Over the last 8+ years Heiland’s career has focused on security research, security assessments, penetration testing, and consulting for corporations and government agencies. Heiland has conducted security research on numerous technical subjects, releasing white papers, security advisories, and presenting at numerous national and international security conferences including Blackhat, Defcon, Shmoocon, DerbyCon, Hackcon Norway, and Hack In Paris. Heiland’s commentary has appeared in several media outlets and publications including ABC World News, Bloomberg UTV, MIT Technical Review, MSNBC, SC Magazine, Threat Post and The Register.

Tom Sellers

Manager, R7 Labs

Tom Sellers is the manager of the Rapid7 Labs team. He has 20 years of experience in IT and Security. He has been responsible for defensive Information Security for companies in the finance, service provider, and security software industries. A strong believer in Open Source, he has contributed to Nmap since 2007 as well as created multiple modules for the Metasploit Project. Today, he and his team leverage efforts such as Projects Sonar and Heisenberg to support research as well as provide insights and context to public.

Kwan Lin

Senior Data Scientist

Kwan is a Senior Data Scientist on the Rapid7 Labs team. His day to day activities often involve analyzing large-scale honeypot or Internet-scan data, applying statistical and machine learning methods to address research or operational challenges, and producing data visualizations to communicate findings to broad audiences. Lead Data Scientist

Roy Hodgman is a Lead Data Scientist at Rapid7 where he works with a team of data scientists to identify, understand and protect against new security threats using machine learing and other statistical techniques. Remembering Jon Hart
1980–2019

Jon Hart, dedicated security professional and researcher, spent the last several years at the helm of Rapid7 Labs’ Project Sonar conducting internet-wide surveys of security issues, the results of which were included in countless vulnerability disclosures, research papers, blog posts, and Rapid7 product capabilities. The security industry as a whole has been fundamentally shaped by his work, and he is truly missed as a colleague and great friend.

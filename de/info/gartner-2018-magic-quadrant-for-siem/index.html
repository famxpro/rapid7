Gartner 2018 Magic Quadrant for Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)

Gartner has named Rapid7 as a Visionary in the 2018 Magic Quadrant for SIEM for the second year in a row, the sole SIEM vendor to achieve this position. Gartner evaluated Rapid7’s SaaS threat detection and response solution, which extends beyond traditional SIEM by including user and entity behavior analytics (UEBA), endpoint detection and response (EDR), and automated threat intelligence, for both the 2018 Magic Quadrant and Critical Capabilities for SIEM.

This layered approach of machine learning and advanced analytics helps customers spot and contain compromise quickly. On the compliance front, Rapid7 includes real-time dashboards and reports to make it easy to highlight security operations progress to business leaders and auditors alike.

In Gartner’s Critical Capabilities for SIEM, we believe we were specifically recognized for User Context and Monitoring, as well as Deployment, Operations, and Support.

About Gartner Magic Quadrant and Critical Capabilities

Gartner Magic Quadrant is a culmination of research in a specific market, giving you a wide-angle view of the relative positions of the market’s competitors. By applying a graphical treatment and a uniform set of evaluation criteria, a Magic Quadrant helps you quickly ascertain how well technology providers are executing their stated visions and how well they are performing against Gartner’s market view. As an essential companion to the Gartner Magic Quadrant, the Critical Capabilities report provides deeper insight into providers’ product and service offerings by extending the Magic Quadrant analysis. Use this research to further investigate product and service ratings based on key capabilities set to important, differentiating use cases.
